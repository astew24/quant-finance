# Additional Project Ideas

## 1. Market Microstructure and Order Book Simulator

Why it stands out:

- Much rarer than another factor notebook
- Directly relevant to quant trading, execution, and HFT-adjacent interviews

Suggested scope:

- replay or simulate limit order book events
- compute imbalance, spread, queue position, and order-flow metrics
- model slippage and short-horizon alpha from microstructure signals
- produce execution-quality metrics like fill ratio and implementation shortfall

## 2. Crypto Statistical Arbitrage Engine

Why it stands out:

- More niche than standard momentum backtests
- Gives the repo a stronger systematic trading angle

Suggested scope:

- build a data pipeline for multi-exchange crypto pairs
- test cointegration and rolling hedge ratios
- implement z-score entry and exit logic with fees and funding adjustments
- report PnL, Sharpe, drawdown, turnover, and regime sensitivity
